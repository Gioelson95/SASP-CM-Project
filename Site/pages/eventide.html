Eventide H9

With the help of Joe Walts from Eventide, has been developed an H9 Max Harmonizer midi controller that control the effects of the fabulous pedal with the movement tracking with Miscrosof Kicect.

H9 send data to the computer, which process messages through Processing, which also is connected to the Microsoft Kinect that track the body of the artist.
